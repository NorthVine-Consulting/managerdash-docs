---
title: Reminders for Career Development
---

This guide covers how to set up and manage customizable reminders for people development activities in ManagerDash.

## Understanding Reminders

Reminders help you stay on top of important people management activities by notifying you when certain development tasks are due. Starting in v1.6.0, ManagerDash includes a customizable reminders system that ensures you maintain regular touchpoints with your team members.

### Why Use Reminders?

Reminders help you:
- Track career development conversations
- Ensure consistent feedback delivery
- Stay proactive about team member growth
- Avoid missing important development milestones

## Types of Reminders

ManagerDash supports customizable reminder types that can be configured for different people development activities:

### Default Reminder Types

- **Performance Reviews**: Quarterly or annual review cycles
- **Career Conversations**: Long-term career development discussions
- **Feedback Check-ins**: Reminder to review and deliver pending feedback
- **Development Goal Reviews**: Progress check on goals and milestone progress

## Managing Reminders

### Viewing Reminders

**Home Dashboard:**
- See all upcoming reminders across your team
- Sorted by due date

### Snoozing a Reminder

If you need to postpone a reminder:

1. Close the reminder by clicking on the X icon
2. The reminder will snooze for 2 days

### Editing reminder cadence

If you want to change a threshold for a reminders:

1. Click on Settings
2. Click on Teams and People
3. Scroll to the "Reminder Rules" section and make changes.
4. Click Save

### Recommended Frequencies

**Career Conversations:**
- Quarterly for growth-focused discussions
- More frequent during promotion cycles

**Feedback Check-ins:**
- Weekly to review pending feedback
- Before scheduled 1-on-1s

**Goal Reviews:**
- Monthly for active goals
- Bi-weekly for complex projects
